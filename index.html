How We Work

Our process is pretty straightforward. Here's how we go from idea to final delivery.

1

Consultation & Script Review

We start by understanding your vision. What are you trying to achieve? What's the budget? Let's talk it through.

2

Pre-Visualization & Planning

Before the cameras roll, we create pre-vis and plan out the VFX shots. This helps everyone stay on the same page.

3

On-Set Capture & Supervision

Our team is on location to supervise the shoot. We make sure all the data is captured properly for post.

4

Post-Production Pipeline

This is where the magic happens. Compositing, CGI, color grading - all of it comes together here.

5

Review & Final Delivery

We send you previews for feedback, make any final tweaks, and deliver in whatever format you need.
